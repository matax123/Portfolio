Ants World

Ants World is an indie videogame of the realtime strategy genre built in Unity.

The game consists in controlling the ants of a colony, make them extract resources from the environment they're immerse and gain terrain.

Inputs are very simple, you move the camera with the arrowkeys and the mouse to select things.

The song at the beginning of the game and the sprites are of my own.
