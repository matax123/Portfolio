Checklist App

Checklist App is a very simple android app made using android studio and coded in java and xml.

Pymes say they have organizational problems when making their internal reportability because the ones destined to do that job are not always in direct contact with the equipment and sales.

This app appears as an alternative to the old internal reportability where the worker informs its job to another worker that would do the report, now the worker are the reporting the job done in situ with their smartphones.
